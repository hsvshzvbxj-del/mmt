# Marketing Initiative Community Platform

## Project Overview

The Marketing Initiative Community Platform is a comprehensive digital platform designed to manage and grow the "Marketing Initiative Community." The platform aims to bring together marketing leaders, specialists, consultants, entrepreneurs, and business owners in a professional environment that fosters knowledge sharing, networking, collaboration, and business opportunities.

# User Roles

## 1. Visitor

Permissions:

* Browse the homepage
* View community goals and vision
* View upcoming events
* Submit a membership application

## 2. Member

Permissions:

* Edit profile
* Create discussions
* Comment on posts
* Like content
* Search members
* View opportunities
* View events
* Register for events

## 3. Moderator

Permissions:

* Review membership applications
* Manage content
* Manage members
* Delete inappropriate posts
* Approve advertisements and opportunities
* Manage events

## 4. Administrator

Full system access and permissions.
